COURSES WE OFFER

Master magic at every level, from beginner charms to advanced alchemical studies. Explore enchanting spells, magical creatures, and ancient wizarding knowledge to unlock your full potential as a skilled and knowledgeable wizard or witch.

Spells & Potions

Discover the art of casting fundamental spells and brewing basic potions. Learn essential incantations, wand techniques, and potion recipes that every aspiring wizard must know to thrive in the magical world.

Magical Creatures & Charms

Dive into the enchanting world of magical beasts and charming spells. Understand the care and handling of creatures while mastering intermediate-level charms for everyday magical tasks and protection.

Advanced Defense & Mystical Arts

Master complex defensive spells and delve into ancient mystical arts. Learn to counter dark magic, explore transfiguration at its highest level, and gain expertise in rare magical disciplines for true wizarding mastery.
